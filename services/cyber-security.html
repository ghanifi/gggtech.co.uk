Why Hotel Security Matters

Hotels are prime targets for cybercriminals due to the high volume of credit card data and personal information they process.

Payment Theft

Unsecured PMS and payment gateways are constant targets for card skimmers and hackers.

Identity Fraud

Guest passport details and personal profiles are high-value assets on the dark web.

Ransomware

A locked system can bring check-ins and operations to a complete standstill.
