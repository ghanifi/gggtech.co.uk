Technology That Works for You

Focus on being a great host while we take care of the technical details. Our B&B solutions are simple to use, highly reliable, and built around the realities of owner-operated UK properties.

Simple Guest WiFi

Secure, easy-to-access WiFi that keeps guests happy without complex login procedures. We set up isolated guest networks so that visitors can browse freely without ever being able to see your own devices or those of other guests. Simple password cards in each room or a basic captive portal provide a polished, professional experience whatever your budget. Bandwidth shaping ensures that one heavy downloader does not ruin connectivity for your other guests.

Booking Engine Support

Ensuring your OTA integrations and direct booking systems are always online and secure is fundamental to your revenue. We configure your network and router settings to prevent the connectivity outages that can desynchronise availability across Booking.com, Airbnb, and your own website. We also provide basic IT support for channel manager platforms popular with UK B&B operators such as Beds24, eviivo, and SuperControl. Our remote helpdesk means booking system problems are resolved quickly, even at unsocial hours.

Expert Remote Support

Instant technical assistance whenever you need it, without the cost of an on-site visit for minor issues. Our UK-based remote support team can diagnose and resolve the majority of B&B IT problems — router issues, printer connectivity, email problems, and payment terminal queries — without ever having to travel to your property. Where a physical visit is required, we schedule it quickly and keep disruption to your guests to an absolute minimum.

IT Challenges Facing UK B&Bs and Guesthouses

Running a B&B or small guesthouse in the UK means wearing every hat simultaneously — front-of-house, housekeeper, accountant, and increasingly, amateur IT support. When the router drops its connection at 10 pm on a Saturday, or the card machine declines a payment on a busy check-in morning, there is usually nobody to call. The cost of a single disrupted booking, a negative review citing poor WiFi, or a failed card payment at a critical moment far outweighs the cost of a professional IT arrangement. Yet many small operators assume that managed IT support is only for large hotels.

GGG Technologies specifically designed its small property offering to address the day-to-day technology pain points of UK B&B owners. We start with a free remote audit of your existing setup, identify the most pressing risks — typically an ageing router without proper firewall functionality, an unsegmented network where guests can see the owner's laptop, or card payment kit that shares the same unsecured broadband connection as everything else — and propose a prioritised, affordable plan of action. Our monthly support plans include proactive monitoring, so we often fix problems before you even know they exist.

Owner-Operator IT Burden

When you are the owner, receptionist, and breakfast chef, troubleshooting a WiFi drop or a failed router update is the last thing you need. Our fully managed approach takes every technology task off your hands — monitoring, patching, backups, and first-line helpdesk — so you can focus entirely on your guests. A single call to our helpdesk is all that is ever needed from your end.

Reliable Card Payments

A declined payment or offline terminal at check-in creates an awkward guest experience and potential revenue loss. We ensure your payment terminal has a reliable, dedicated network connection and is correctly isolated from your guest WiFi to satisfy PCI DSS scope requirements. We also advise on 4G failover options so that a broadband outage never prevents you from taking payment.

Guest WiFi Network Isolation

On a typical domestic router, guests sharing your WiFi can see other connected devices — including your laptop, NAS drives, and smart home equipment. We install proper VLAN-segregated networks that put every guest on an isolated segment, preventing one guest from accidentally (or maliciously) accessing another guest's device or your own business data. This also protects you legally and reputationally should a guest misuse the connection.

IT Services for B&Bs & Guesthouses

Practical, affordable IT services tailored to the scale and budget of owner-operated UK bed and breakfast properties.

Network & WiFi Solutions

Properly segmented guest and owner networks using affordable, reliable hardware suited to small properties. We ensure every guest room has strong signal and that your own business devices are always protected from the guest zone.

IT Support & Maintenance

Remote monitoring and helpdesk access on a fixed monthly cost — no surprise call-out bills. Our support team is UK-based and understands the pressures of running a small hospitality business, so responses are prompt and plain-English.

PCI DSS Compliance

Even a small guesthouse taking card payments must meet PCI DSS requirements. We handle network segmentation, SAQ completion guidance, and the technical controls required by your acquirer — making compliance straightforward and affordable for small operators.

PMS & Booking Systems

Support for the cloud-based booking and channel management platforms used by UK B&Bs, including eviivo, Beds24, and Little Hotelier. We ensure connectivity to OTAs is stable and that your availability and rates are always synced correctly.

Cyber Security

Small guesthouses are increasingly targeted by phishing scams and fake booking fraud. We deploy firewall protection, email filtering, and provide straightforward security guidance to protect your reservations inbox and financial accounts from compromise.

Data Cabling

Where wireless performance alone is insufficient — particularly in multi-storey properties or those with thick internal walls — we install neat, tidy data cabling runs to bring wired connectivity to access points or key areas, improving reliability for the whole property.
