Our Journey

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leaders

Founded in 2010, GGG Technologies started with a simple mission: to provide exceptional IT support tailored specifically for the hospitality industry. We saw that hotels were being underserved by generic IT providers who didn't understand the unique challenges of running a hotel.

Fast forward to today, and we've become one of the UK's most trusted hotel IT specialists, serving over 500 properties nationwide. From boutique B&Bs to large chain hotels, we've helped transform technology operations and guest experiences.
